Algorithm: Discord Candidate

Authors: Dragomir Yankov, Eamonn Keogh and Umaa Rebbapragada

Original paper: Disk Aware Discord Discovery: Finding Unusual Time Series in Terabyte Sized Datasets

Website: http://www.cs.ucr.edu/~eamonn/selected_publications.htm

Description: This method counts euclidean distances between each time series and choose k-farest. The time series data may cross files.
