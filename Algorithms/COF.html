• Algorithm: Connectivity-based Outlier Factor (COF)
  • Authors: Jian Tang, Zhixiang Chen, Ada Wai-Chee Fu, David Wai-Lok Cheung
  • Original paper: "Enhancing Effectiveness of Outlier Detections for Low Density Patterns"
  • Description: COF is a modification of LOF which is designed to recognize outliers which are unconnected to their neighbouring points, and to recognize points which can be connected to neighbours as normal even if they reside in low density spaces per the original LOF calculation.
